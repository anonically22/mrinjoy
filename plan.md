# MRINJOY Partners Website — Launch Readiness & Compliance Plan

## Project Overview

Website Type:

* Intellectual Property Law Firm Website
* Lead Generation & Trust Building Platform
* Informational Website (No client portal)

Primary Goals:

1. Establish credibility and authority.
2. Showcase IP legal services.
3. Generate qualified consultation inquiries.
4. Educate visitors through FAQs and service pages.
5. Maintain compliance with Indian privacy requirements.

---

# Phase 1 — Pre-Launch Requirements (Mandatory)

## Legal & Compliance

### Privacy Policy

Create a dedicated Privacy Policy page.

Must include:

* Information collected through forms
* Purpose of collection
* Data retention practices
* User rights
* Contact information
* Third-party services used
* Security measures

Footer Link Required:

```text
Privacy Policy
```

---

### Terms of Use

Create a dedicated Terms of Use page.

Must include:

* Website usage conditions
* Intellectual property ownership
* Limitation of liability
* Governing law
* Contact information

Footer Link Required:

```text
Terms of Use
```

---

### Legal Disclaimer

Create a dedicated Disclaimer page.

Must clearly state:

* Website content is informational only
* No legal advice is being provided
* Visiting the website does not create an attorney-client relationship
* Results discussed do not guarantee future outcomes
* Content may not apply to every legal situation

Footer Link Required:

```text
Disclaimer
```

# Contact Form Compliance

---

## Add Consent Checkbox

Required before submission.

Text:

"I consent to MRINJOY Partners using my information to respond to my inquiry."

Submission should be blocked unless checked.

## Add Data Collection Notice

Display below the form.

Text:

"Your information is collected solely for responding to your inquiry and providing requested services."

---

## Form Validation

Required:

* Full Name required
* Email required
* Valid email format
* Subject required
* Message required
* Message minimum length
* Message maximum length

# Content Review

## Remove Potentially Risky Marketing Claims

Current Statistic:

```text
98% Success Rate
```

Recommended Replacement:

```text
100+ Clients Served
500+ Cases Handled
4+ Years Experience
Pan-India Advisory
```

Reason:
Avoid implying guaranteed legal outcomes.
